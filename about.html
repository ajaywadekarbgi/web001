Mr. Ajay Wadekar
Assistant Professor, Department of Computer Science and Engineering Bansal Group of Institutions, Bhopal

Mr. Ajay Wadekar has over 12 years of experience in the field of Information Technology and Technical Training.

He is currently pursuing his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT), Bhopal.

Throughout his professional journey, he has trained 5,000+ students in technologies like C, C++, Java, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, PHP, Data Structures, Algorithms, Data Science, and Machine Learning.

He is a GATE and NET qualified professional, and a 5-time NPTEL certified educator. Passionate about education and innovation, he constantly mentors students toward excellence in programming, research, and career development.

Additionally, Mr. Wadekar has served as a jury member in Hackathons and IEEE events at MANIT Bhopal and other reputed institutes, contributing to the growth of young minds in technology and innovation.
